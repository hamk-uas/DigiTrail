DigiTrail Project and project goal

DigiTrail is a project of the European Agricultural Fund for Rural Development funded by the Häme ELY Centre (Centre for Economic Development, Transport and the Environment) and running from 1 August 2016 to 30 June 2019. DigiTrail enhances the accessibility and visibility of Kanta-Häme nature trails by digital means. Nature trails in the following locations have been selected for development in the project: Aulanko, Evo, Tammela and Loppi. In addition, Hämeen Härkätie (Ox Road of Häme) has been selected as a cycling route for further development.
